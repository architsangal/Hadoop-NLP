[ September 2010 ] This article lists political parties in Sweden. Sweden has a multi-party system with numerous political parties, in which no one party often has a chance of gaining power alone, and parties must work with each other to form coalition government s. [ The parties ] The letter or letters in brackets after each Swedish party name are the abbreviations commonly used for those parties in the Swedish media.
