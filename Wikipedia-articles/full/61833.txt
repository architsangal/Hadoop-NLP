[ January 2010 ] [ Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences United States http://www.oscars.org ] This name for the Academy Award for Live Action Short Film was introduced in 1974. For the three preceding years it was known as &quot; Short Subjects, Live Action Films. &quot; The term &quot; Short Subjects, Live Action Subjects &quot; was used from 1957 until 1970. From 1936 until 1956 there were two separate awards, &quot; Best Short Subject, One-reel &quot; and &quot; Best Short Subject, Two-reel &quot;. A third category &quot; Best Short Subject, color &quot; was used only for 1936 and 1937. From the initiation of short subject awards for 1932 until 1935 the terms were &quot; Best Short Subject, comedy &quot; and &quot; Best Short Subject, novelty &quot;.
