[ Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal quality rock song performances by a duo or group with vocals Grammy.jpg 100px A gold gramophone trophy with a plaque set on a table Gilded gramophone trophy presented to Grammy Award winners National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences United States 1980 2011 ] The Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal was awarded between 1980 and 2011. The award was discontinued after the 2011 award season in a major overhaul of Grammy categories. From 2012, all solo or duo/group performances (including instrumental performance) in the rock category will be shifted to the newly formed Best Rock Performance category. U2 hold the record for most awards with a total of seven, followed by Aerosmith who have won four times.
