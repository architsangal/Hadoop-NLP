Paris_9e_arr_jms.gif Paris and its closest suburbs Île-de-France Paris Jacques Bravo 2.18 55,838 58,500 8 March 1999 census 2005 ] The 9th arrondissement (IX e arrondissement), located on the Right Bank, is one of the 20 arrondissements of Paris, France. It contains many places of cultural, historical, and architectural interest, including the Palais Garnier, home to the Paris Opera, Boulevard Haussmann and its large department stores of Galeries Lafayette and Printemps. Along with the 2nd and 8th arrondissements, it hosts one of the business centers of Paris, located around the Opéra. [ Geography ] The land area of this arrondissement is 2.179  km 2 (0.841 sq. miles, or 538 acres).
