[ Sioux County Nebraska 1877 Harrison Harrison 2067 2067 1 0.04% 2010 1311 0 Sioux County, Nebraska courthouse from E.JPG Sioux County Courthouse in Harrison, Nebraska ] Sioux County is a county located in the U.S. state of Nebraska. As of 2010, the population was 1,311. { http://www.rapidcityjournal.com/thechadronnews/news/article_0bfc2de8-943e-5790-a008-f492b9e21542.html } Its county seat is Harrison [ 6 ]. In the Nebraska license plate system, Sioux County is represented by the prefix 80 (it had the 80th-largest number of vehicles registered in the state when the license plate system was established in 1922). [ Geography ] According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 2,067  square miles (5,354  km²), of which 2,067  square miles (5,352  km²) is land and 1  square mile (2  km²) (0.04%) is water.
