400 px There are 9 cities ( [ Republikas pilsētas ] ) and 67 towns ( [ Novada pilsētas ] ) in Latvia. By Latvian law town s are settlements that are centers of culture and commerce with well-developed engineer-infrastructure and street grid, and have at least 2,000 residents; however, a settlement can be designated a town, if it has fewer residents, but fulfils all other requirements. To become a city, a town must have at least 25,000 residents, additionally city should have well-developed commerce, transport, public utilities, social infrastructure and be significant center of culture, however these requirements may be disregarded if there is sufficient population. { Law On Administrative Territories and Populated Areas } [ Cities ] [ Lv-Daugavpils.ogg pronunciation ] [
