This is a list of Norwegian governments with parties and Prime Ministers. Within coalition governments the parties are listed according to parliamentary representation with the most popular party first. The Prime Ministers&apos; parties are italicized. [ Governments ] [ Between 1814 and 1884 ] With exception of the Cabinet of 1814, that was appointed by the then Crown-Prince of Denmark, Christian Fredrik, all the cabinets were appointed by the King of Sweden, who was also King of Norway. Johan Nygaardsvold Labour [ De facto Governments during World War II ] During the German occupation of Norway during World War II there were four cabinets, that ruled as part of Josef Terboven s administration of Norway. These Governments were the de facto ruling body of Norway during the war, though the Cabinet Nygaardsvold still held the de jure office, in exile in London, United Kingdom.
