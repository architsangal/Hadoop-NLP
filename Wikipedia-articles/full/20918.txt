The area known as the &quot; Near East &quot; is usually referred to as Middle East in modern contexts.For periods predating Classical Antiquity, the common term is Ancient Near East.The Near East is generally associated with Anatolia, the Levant, Mesopotamia and to greater degree Egypt, Arabia and Greater Persia.
