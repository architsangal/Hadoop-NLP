[ February 2011 ] [ 1338 ] [ 1338 ] { NOTOC } Year 1338 ( MCCCXXXVIII ) was a common year starting on Thursday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. [ Events ] [ Date unknown ] Hundred Years&apos; War : Louis IV, Holy Roman Emperor appoints Edward III of England as a vicar-general of the Holy Roman Empire. Louis supports Edward&apos;s claim to the French throne under the terms of the Treaty of Koblenz. Philip VI of France besieges Guienne in Southwest France and his navy attacks Portsmouth in England. Ashikaga Takauji is granted the title of Shogun by the emperor of Japan, starting the Ashikaga shogunate. Nicomedia is captured by the Ottoman Empire.
