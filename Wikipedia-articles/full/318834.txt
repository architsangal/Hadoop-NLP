left At the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, eight events in fencing were contested. Men competed in both individual and team events for each of the three weapon types ( épée, foil and sabre ), but women competed only in foil events. [ http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/summer/1984/FEN/ Fencing: 1984 Olympic Results 2011-04-26 sports-reference.com ] They were held between August 1 and 11 at the Long Beach Convention Center.
