A map of the Philippines showing the island groups of Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao This is a list of island s of the Philippines. The Philippine archipelago comprises 7,107 islands, of which only about 2,000 are inhabited. { Magical Islands, Philippine Tourism, retrieved 2012 } They are clustered into the three major island groups of Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The following list breaks the islands down further by region and smaller island group for easier reference.
