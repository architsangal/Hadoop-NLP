[ History ] The Southern Pacific Railroad reached Bradley in 1886, and a post office was established the same year. The name honors Bradley V. Sargent, who owned the land on which the railway station was built. [ Demographics ] [ 2010 ] The 2010 United States Census reported that Bradley had a population of 93.
