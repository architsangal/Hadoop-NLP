[ Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album quality albums in the alternative music genre Grammy.jpg 100px A gold gramophone trophy with a plaque set on a table Gilded gramophone trophy presented to Grammy Award winners National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences United States 1991 2012 ] The Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album is an award presented to recording artists for quality albums in the alternative rock genre at the Grammy Award s, a ceremony that was established in 1958 and originally called the Gramophone Awards. [ http://theenvelope.latimes.com/awards/grammys/env-grammy_awards_info%2C0%2C5279018.htmlstory?track%3Dcenter Grammy Awards at a Glance Los Angeles Times Tribune Company April 29, 2010 ] Honors in several categories are presented at the ceremony annually by the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences of the United States to &quot; honor artistic achievement, technical proficiency and overall excellence in the recording industry, without regard to album sales or chart position &quot;. [ http://www2.grammy.com/Recording_Academy/ Overview April 29, 2010 National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences ] While the definition of &quot; alternative &quot; has been debated, [ http://today.msnbc.msn.com/id/10993760/ Alternative to what? January 23, 2006 April 29, 2010 Helen A.S. Popkin msnbc.com ] the award was first presented in 1991 to recognize non-mainstream rock albums &quot; heavily played on college radio stations &quot;. [ http://news.google.com/newspapers?id%3DlFkeAAAAIBAJ%26amp%3Bsjid%3DZ8gEAAAAIBAJ%26amp%3Bpg%3D1092%2C4464120%26amp%3Bdq Grammys return to New York May 25, 1990 TimesDaily Tennessee Valley Printing April 29, 2010 ] [ http://www.nytimes.com/1991/01/11/arts/grammy-nominees-announced.html Grammy Nominees Announced January 11, 1991 April 29, 2010 The New York Times The New York Times Company Jon Pareles Jon Pareles ] According to the category description guide for the 52nd Grammy Awards, the award is presented to &quot; vocal or instrumental alternative music albums containing at least 51% playing time of newly recorded music &quot;, defining &quot; alternative &quot; as a &quot; non-traditional &quot; genre that exists &quot; outside of the mainstream music consciousness &quot;. [ http://www2.grammy.com/PDFs/Recording_Academy/52guide.pdf 52nd OEP Category Description Guide May 25, 2010 2 PDF National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences ] In 1991, and from 1994 to 1999, the award was known as Best Alternative Music Performance. Beginning in 2001, award recipients included the producers, engineers, and/or mixers associated with the nominated work in addition to the recording artists. [ http://www2.grammy.com/GRAMMY_Awards/Winners/Results.aspx Grammy Award Winners National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences April 29, 2010 ] { Note: User must select the &quot; Alternative &quot; category as the genre under the search feature. } As of 2012, Radiohead and The White Stripes share the record for the most wins in this category, having won three times each. With seven nominations to date, Radiohead holds the record for the most nominations in this category, plus Radiohead lead singer, Thom Yorke, was also nominated for the 2007 award for his solo album. Beck holds the record for the most nominations for a solo artist, with six. Beck and Coldplay have each received the award twice, the latter being the only group to win two years consecutively. American artists have been presented with the award more than any other nationality, though it has been presented to musicians or groups from the United Kingdom five times, from Ireland twice, and from France once. Female musicians Tori Amos and Björk hold the record for the most nominations without a win, with five nominations each. Death Cab for Cutie holds the record for the most nominations for a group without a win, with four nominations.
