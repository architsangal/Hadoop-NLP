There are number of islands around Sri Lanka. The most prominent islets are west of the Jaffna Peninsula in the Northern Province. These group of islands also had Dutch names during the Dutch colonial period but only a few of those names are still in use today. { Dutch heritage of Sri Lanka } [ February 2011 ] The old Parkrit-Pali-Sinhala names, found in the Pali chronicles like the Mahavamsa, have been discussed by toponymists and historians. { Place-names in Sri lanka } Although they are infrequently used, we have included them in the table (under notes) due to their touristic and historical interest.
