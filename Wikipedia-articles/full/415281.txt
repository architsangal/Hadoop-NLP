DeGregorio, Jr. 2003 http://www.atlantichockeyonline.com/ #0047AB #FFFFFF Map - College Hockey - Atlantic Hockey states.svg 250 ] The Atlantic Hockey Association is a NCAA Men&apos;s Division I Ice Hockey conference which operates primarily in the northeastern United States. It participates in the NCAA &apos;s Division I as an ice hockey –only conference. Unlike several other college athletic conference s, Atlantic Hockey has no women&apos;s division, though it shares some organizational and administrative roles (and three universities) with the women&apos;s-only College Hockey America. From 1997 to 2003, Atlantic Hockey was sponsored by the Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference (MAAC).
