{ Uzeyir Hajibeyov } { Ahmad Javad } { 1918 } { Azərbaycan Respublikasının Dövlət Himni instrumental.ogg } { Azərbaycan Respublikasının Dövlət Himni (Instrumental) } Azərbaycan Marşı is the national anthem of Azerbaijan, the original title of which is March of Azerbaijan (Azərbaycan marşı). The words were written by the poet Ahmad Javad, and the music was composed by Azeri composer Uzeyir Hajibeyov. The anthem was adopted in 1918 with the passage of &quot; On the State Hymn of the Republic of Azerbaijan. &quot; { &apos;&apos;THE NATIONAL ANTHEM&apos;&apos; - THE NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF THE REPUBLIC OF AZERBAIJAN - Azerbaijan.az.
