It borders (clockwise from the West) on the Walloon provinces of Hainaut, Walloon Brabant, Liège and Luxembourg in Belgium, and on France. Its capital is the city of Namur. It has an area of 3,664  km² and is divided into three administrative districts (arrondissements in French ) containing 38 local authorities (communes in French ).
