[ February 2012 ] [ 1978 3 21 y ] { Mumbai, } { Maharashtra, } { India } { 1997–present } { Film actress } { Rani Mukherji Rani Mukerjee } Rani Mukerji (born Rani Mukherjee on 21 March 1978) [ Wish Rani Mukerji! 19 March 2008 http://www.rediff.com/movies/2008/mar/19wish.htm Rediff.com 5 January 2011 ] is an Indian film actress who works in Hindi movies. Born to the Mukherjee-Samarth family, she made her acting debut with a cameo appearance in the Bengali film Biyer Phool (1996), on the insistence of her mother. The following year, Mukerji made her Hindi film debut with Raja Ki Aayegi Baraat, and subsequently had her first, and still biggest commercial success with Karan Johar &apos;s romance Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998), which won her a Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actress. She later starred in several films that fared below expectations, before featuring in the critically acclaimed drama Saathiya, for which she won her first Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actress. [ Gangadhar, V. The Tribune Superstars 5 February 2005 http://www.tribuneindia.com/2005/20050205/saturday/main1.htm 11 February 2008 ] In 2004, her performances in the hit romantic comedy Hum Tum and the critically acclaimed Yuva earned her the Best Actress and Best Supporting Actress awards, respectively, at the 50th Filmfare Awards. She later received unanimous praise for her portrayal of a blind, deaf and mute woman in Black (2005), for which she garnered two more Filmfare Awards: Best Actress and Actress - Critics. She went on to play leading roles in the comedy Bunty Aur Babli (2005) and the adult drama Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna (2006), both of which emerged as major commercial successes in India as well as overseas. She is a regular stage performer and has been involved in philanthropic endeavors related to women and children. Despite constant speculation, she remains guarded about her personal life and is sometimes labelled a recluse by members of the media. Mukerji currently lives in Juhu with her parents. [ Early life and background ] [ Mukherjee-Samarth family ] Mukerji was born in Mumbai on 21 March 1978 to a film-oriented family of Bengal i origin. Her father, Ram Mukherjee, is a retired director and one of the founders of Filmalaya Studios while her mother, Krishna, was a playback singer. [ Rediff.com My sister, Rani 22 August 2002 http://www.rediff.com/movies/2002/aug/22rani.htm 12 January 2012 ] Her maternal aunt, Debashree Roy, is a Bengali film actress and her cousin, Kajol, is a popular Bollywood actress.
