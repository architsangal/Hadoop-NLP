[ Pulaski County Illinois 150 1843 Mound City 203.23 199.18 4.04 1.99% 2010 6161 15 ] Pulaski County is a county located on the Ohio River in the U.S. state of Illinois. According to the 2010 census, it has a population of 6,161, which is a decrease of 16.2% from 7,348 in 2000. [ http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/states/17/17153.html Pulaski County QuickFacts 2011-11-05 United States Census Bureau ] Its county seat is Mound City. [ 6 ] [ History ] Pulaski County was formed on March 3, 1843, out of parts of Alexander and Johnson Counties. It was named in honor of Kazimierz Pułaski who was killed at the Siege of Savannah in the Revolutionary War.
