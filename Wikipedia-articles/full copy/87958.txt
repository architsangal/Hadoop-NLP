[ History ] Santa Cruz County, formed on March 15, 1899, out of what was then Pima County, is named after the Santa Cruz River, which was named in the late 17th century by Father Kino. Santa Cruz means &quot; holy cross &quot; in Spanish. After that Father Kino built the famous mission which still stands today at the Tumacácori National Historical Park.
