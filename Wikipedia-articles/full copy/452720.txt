right Kreis Koschmin ( [ Powiat Koźmiński ] ) was a county in the southern administrative district of Posen, in the Prussian province of Posen. It presently lies in the southern part of Polish region of Greater Poland Voivodeship. [ Military command ] Kreis Koschmin was part of the military command ( [ Bezirkskommando ] ) in Posen at Kosten. [ Court system ] The main court ( [ Landgericht ] ) was in Lissa, with lower courts ( [ Amtsgericht ] ) in Koschmin and Krotoschin.
