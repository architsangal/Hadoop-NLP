Map of Niger { Niamey, Capital of } { Niger } This is a list of cities and towns in Niger. All larger cities are also Communes of Niger. While often translated as &quot; town &quot;, Nigerien communes are simply the third level administrative subdivision of the nation. These can be classified Urban or Rural communes, and while often the administrative unit of a town or city, all areas of the country fall within a commune. Smaller towns and neighborhoods are designated Quarters (Urban) or Villages (Rural). [ Largest cities and urban centers ] Cities with population over 10,000, according to 2001 census.
