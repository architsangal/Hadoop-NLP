As of 2010, the population was 65,645. Its county seat is Roswell. [ 6 ] The name commemorates the name of Jose Francisco Chaves. Created by Territorial Legislature, Feb. 25, 1889, out of land from the county of Lincoln. The county&apos;s area comprises 6,071 sq mi, making it the fourth largest county in the state.
