They were held in Tokyo, Japan, they were the last Summer Paralympics to take place in the same city as the Summer Olympics until the 1988 Summer Paralympics. The term &quot; Paralympic Games &quot; was approved by the International Olympic Committee (IOC) first in 1984, { Rome 1960, } { International Paralympic Committee (IPC) } while the International Paralympic Committee (IPC) was formed in 1989. In contrast with the 1960 Games, many events had more than three participants, meaning that athletes were no longer guaranteed a medal upon completing their event. { IPC searchable database } Also originally known as Paralympic Tokyo 1964. { History and Use of the Term Paralympic, } { International Paralympic Committee (IPC) } [ Sports ] Nine sports were competed at the 1964 games. In athletics, a wheelchair racing event in the form of a 60 m dash was added; previously the athletics program had included only field events. Wheelchair racing has since become one of the most prominent Paralympic events.
