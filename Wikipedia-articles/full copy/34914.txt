Barthelemy Catherine Joubert, later general, becomes sub-lieutenant. Johann Georg Albrechtberger becomes Kapellmeister in Vienna. The State Street Corporation is founded. Shiloh Meeting House, predecessor of Shiloh United Methodist Church in Lynchburg, Virginia, is founded.
