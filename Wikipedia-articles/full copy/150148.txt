[ May 2011 ] [ 44th Grammylogo44.png 125px February 27, 2002 Staples Center, Los Angeles, California Jon Stewart CBS 2003 2001 ] The 44th Grammy Awards were held on February 27, 2002. The biggest was Alicia Keys, winning five Grammys, including Best New Artist and Song of the Year for &quot; Fallin&apos; &quot;. U2 won four awards including Record of the Year and Best Rock Album.
