It is located on the Trans-Canada Highway approximately [ 140 km mi ] east of Calgary and [ 160 km mi ] northwest of Medicine Hat. It is also on the main line of the Canadian Pacific Railway. A short distance south of the town is the Bassano Dam, which serves as a reservoir for nearby communities and for the irrigation of the vast farm and ranch lands surrounding Bassano.
