St. Anthony is a city in and the county seat of Fremont County, Idaho, United States. [ 6 ] The population was 3,542 at the 2010 census, up from 3,342 in 2000. { Spokesman-Review - 2010 census - St. Anthony, Idaho - accessed 2011-12-26 } It is part of the Rexburg, Idaho Micropolitan Statistical Area. [ Geography ] St. Anthony is located at [ 43 57 58 N 111 41 4 W type:city ] (43.966079, -111.684480), [ 1 ] at an elevation of [ 4967 ft m ] above sea level. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of [ 1.3 sqmi km2 ], of which, [ 1.3 sqmi km2 ] of it is land and 0.86% is water. [ Demographics ] [ 411 1237 2957 2778 2719 2695 2700 2877 3212 2010 3342 3542 source: Moffatt, Riley. Population History of Western U.S. Cities &amp; Towns, 1850-1990. Lanham : Scarecrow, 1996, 98. ] As of the census [ 2 ] of 2000, there were 3,342 people, 1,091 households, and 819 families residing in the city. The population density was 2,565.0 people per square mile (992.6/km²). There were 1,218 housing units at an average density of 934.8 per square mile (361.7/km²). The racial makeup of the city was 89.05% White, 0.21% African American, 0.69% Native American, 0.66% Asian, 0.06% Pacific Islander, 7.09% from other races, and 2.24% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 15.38% of the population. There were 1,091 households out of which 40.1% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 59.9% were married couples living together, 11.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 24.9% were non-families. 22.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 10.1% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.94 and the average family size was 3.47. In the city the population was spread out with 33.2% under the age of 18, 10.1% from 18 to 24, 26.8% from 25 to 44, 18.5% from 45 to 64, and 11.4% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 30 years. For every 100 females there were 107.6 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 109.8 males. The median income for a household in the city was $31,023, and the median income for a family was $37,995. Males had a median income of $26,625 versus $22,734 for females. The per capita income for the city was $12,898. About 10.3% of families and 15.6% of the population were below the poverty line, including 20.8% of those under age 18 and 14.2% of those age 65 or over.
