[ Early life and career ] Carey was born in Liverpool, England, in 1959 - describing his young self as &quot; one of those ominously quiet kids... [who] lived so much inside my own head I only had vestigial limbs &quot;. As a child, he maintained an interest in comics, writing and drawing primitive stories to entertain his younger brother. [ Carey Mike On The Ledge DC Comics / Vertigo July 2000 ] He studied English at St Peter&apos;s College, Oxford [ http://www.theoxfordforum.com/pdf/Oxford%20Forum%20Issue%203.pdf pdf Oxford Forum Autumn 2005 Comic Stripped Carey Mike 54 –55 27 July 2009 ] before becoming a teacher. He continued to teach for 15 years before moving on to writing comics. [ Writing career ] After a series of one-off jobs for independent comics companies, including a biographical comic of Ozzy Osbourne and a fantasy about the band Pantera, Carey gained regular employment at 2000 AD, where he created the original series Th1rt3en and Carver Hale. For the Vertigo imprint of DC Comics Carey went on to write the entire run of the Eisner Award -nominated comic book Lucifer, [ Irvine Alex Alexander C.
