This is a complete list of basilicas of the Roman Catholic Church. A basilica is a church with certain privileges conferred on it by the Pope. Not all churches with &quot; basilica &quot; in their title actually have the ecclesiastical status, which can lead to confusion, since it is also an architectural term for a church-building style.
