Yahoo! http://www.webcitation.org/65dHBOG3S 2012-02-22 ] Slowhand is a studio album by English recording artist Eric Clapton, released in November 1977 on RSO Records. The title of the album is Eric&apos;s nickname which was given to him by Giorgio Gomelsky. The album peaked at number two on the US &apos;&apos;Billboard&apos;&apos; 200, and it produced three hit single s on the Hot 100, &quot; Lay Down Sally &quot;, &quot; Wonderful Tonight &quot;, and &quot; Cocaine &quot;. In 2003, the album was ranked number 325 on Rolling Stone s list of the 500 greatest albums of all time.
